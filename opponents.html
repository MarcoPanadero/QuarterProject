JOGO

Jogo is one of the disaster curses, his cursed technique is disaster flames which he uses in a number of ways to burn his enemies to a crisp, Gojo encountered him on a road where Jogo was easily beaten.

SUKUNA

Sukuna is the so called "King of Curses" due to his overwhelming strength and powerful cursed technique, he encountered Gojo many times but on their last encounter Sukuna got the upper hand and killed Gojo.

HANAMI

Hanami is another one of the disaster curses, her cursed technique is disaster plants which she uses to pierce and trap opponents with, Gojo encountered her alongside Jogo where Gojo easily defeated her.
