Geto Suguru

Suguru used to be Gojo Satoru's best friend, both of them together were considered to be "the strongest", but that all changed when Geto betrayed the school and Gojo was forced to kill him.

Ieiri Shoko

Shoko was a classmate of Satoru and Suguru, she wasn't strong but had the power to heal other people, after the incident with Geto she became depressed and distant.

First Years

The first years are Gojos students consisting of Fushiguro Megumi, Itadori Yuji, and Kugisaki Nobara, Gojo taught them how to be good sorcerers so that they wouldn't perish in the future.
